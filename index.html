About Winter Workshop

Winter Workshop is a yearly event that brings together enthusiasts of winter sports and activities. Whether you love skiing, snowboarding, or simply enjoying the beauty of winter landscapes, this workshop is the perfect opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals.

Event Details

Date: January 15th - 20th, 20XX

Location: Snowy Peaks Resort, Winter Wonderland

Activities

  • Skiing and Snowboarding lessons
  • Snowshoeing adventures
  • Ice skating on the frozen lake
  • Winter photography workshops
